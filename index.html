HOW TO FIGHT BACK

1

Get a Wallet

Download MetaMask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google chrome extension store for free.

2

Get Some ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to swap for $BBC. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on MetaMask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

3

Go to Uniswap

Connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in your browser or within your MetaMask app's browser. Connect your wallet.

4

Swap for $BBC

Paste the $BBC token address, select $BBC, and confirm. When MetaMask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
